What is an INC Grade?

An INC grade is given to a student who has not completed all the required coursework for a class by the end of the semester. This grade is not calculated in the student's GPA, but it does affect their academic standing. The student has one year to complete the coursework and have the grade changed to a letter grade. If the student does not complete the coursework within the year, the INC grade will be changed to an F.
